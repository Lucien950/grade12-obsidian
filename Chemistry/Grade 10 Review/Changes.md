# Changes
## Physical Changes
> Rearranging atoms
- Always Completely reversable (reversable $\ne$ physical change)
- **Substance not altered**
- Ex
	- Heat
	- Ripping
	- Mixing
## Chemical Changes
- **Fundemental Substance Altered**
- Usually unreversable (lot's of times reversable)
- Ex
	- Reactions
- Signs
	- Colour Change
	- Precipitate
	- New Smell
	- Energy transfer (light, heat)
